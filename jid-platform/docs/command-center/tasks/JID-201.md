# JID-201 — Opportunity-to-Decision Journey

## Objective

Prove the end-to-end owned-Profile opportunity journey from creation through application/declaration, radar, employer pipeline, and final decision with correct entitlements and tenant privacy.

## Business reason

The platform is usable only when a verified Business can operate a real opportunity and an Individual can move through it without document leakage or cross-tenant access.

## Constitutional sources

- Constitution Articles 2, 4, 5, 6, 7, 9, 10, and 11.
- Feature Ledger `OPP-01`, `APP-01`, `PIPE-01`, and `BILL-01` in [FEATURE_LEDGER.md](../FEATURE_LEDGER.md).
- [TASK_BOARD.md](../TASK_BOARD.md).

## Prerequisites

- JID-102 has closed owned organizational identity and operational anchoring.
- JID-103 has closed individual projections and privacy read paths.
- Inventory final job/application schemas, status guards, entitlements, APIs, and UI journeys before editing.

## Dependency gates

- JID-102: `CODE_COMPLETE` and independently verified green.
- JID-103: `CODE_COMPLETE` and independently verified green.

## Scope

- Create, edit, publish, close, search, and view opportunities anchored to owned Business Profiles.
- Enforce Normal/Plus entitlements from real plan data.
- Complete application or self-declaration through Individual radar and Business pipeline to decision.
- Enforce zero-document hiring, server-side privacy, and cross-tenant denial.
- Provide honest loading/empty/error/forbidden states and AR/EN parity.

## Out of scope

- Payment-provider integration, speculative scoring, SSIS provider work, communications automation, document upload, or Directory anchoring.

## Environment

- Isolated approved non-production branch/worktree; local app and explicitly authorized disposable local database for write tests.
- No production, `main`, cloud database, Vercel deployment, shared local stack, or secrets without a separately approved packet.

## Permissions

- Opportunity/application/pipeline/entitlement code, tests, documentation, and strictly required forward-only migrations are allowed.
- No production or remote writes.

## Expected file domains

- Business opportunity creation/management, public opportunity search/detail, Individual application/declaration/radar, Business applicant pipeline, entitlement helpers, focused tests/migrations, and `docs/command-center/reports/JID-201_REPORT.md`.

## Tests

- Owned-Profile creation/publish/close and Normal/Plus gate matrix.
- Individual application/declaration lifecycle and decision visibility.
- Cross-tenant read/write denial, private-field absence, duplicate/invalid transition denial, and zero documents.
- AR/EN, keyboard/mobile shell states, focused unit/integration/E2E/RLS tests, `git diff --check`, type-check, lint, and build.

## Acceptance criteria

- One real-data journey succeeds end to end for authorized actors.
- Unauthorized tenants receive no operational/private data.
- Every displayed tier, count, and status has a traceable source and absent data disappears cleanly.
- All required gates pass with no skipped security assertions.

## Evidence

- Report changed files, reused/new code, state-transition sources, entitlement sources, privacy payloads, journey traces, gaps, and exact validation output.

## Stop conditions

- Stop if either dependency is not verified green, a required real source is absent, a constitutional privacy/zero-document conflict appears, remote access is required, or the same repair fails twice.

## Executor

Codex on the designated JID-201 non-production branch.

## Verifier

Independent security reviewer and command center.

## Final-state vocabulary

Final line: `CODE_COMPLETE` or `BLOCKED_WITH_EXACT_CAUSE`.
